Our Story

Internet built by your neighbours, for your neighbourhood

Krishbyl Broadband started with a simple frustration — slow speeds, hidden charges, and support teams that never picked up. We set out to do it differently.

Today we run a modern fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) network that brings true high-speed internet to homes, shops, and businesses. We keep our pricing transparent, our data truly unlimited, and our support genuinely local — so help is always close by.

  • 100% fiber-optic network — no copper, no compromises
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden charges
  • Local engineers and same-day support
  • Continuously expanding coverage across the region
