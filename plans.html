FAQ

Plan questions, answered

Are there any hidden charges?

No. Installation and the Wi-Fi router are free. You only pay the plan price plus applicable GST.

Is the data really unlimited?

Yes. There is no FUP and no data cap. You get full speed for the entire billing cycle.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can change your plan at the start of any billing cycle — just reach out to support.

How long does installation take?

In most serviceable areas, installation is completed within 24–48 hours of feasibility confirmation.

What if my area isn't covered yet?

Submit the new connection form anyway — we are expanding fast and will notify you the moment your area goes live.

Found your plan?

Apply online in under two minutes. Our team will call you to confirm and schedule installation.
